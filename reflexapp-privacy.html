Privacy Policy for ReactionTest

Last Updated: March 10, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the ReactionTest (also referred to as "ReflexApp") mobile application (herein referred to as "Application").

1. Information Collection and Use

The Application is designed to prioritize user privacy. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) to external servers. All data generated within the Application, including test results, reaction times, and user profiles, is stored locally on your device.

2. Local Data Storage

The Application uses Hive and SharedPreferences to store your training history, high scores, and level progress locally. This data never leaves your device and is only used to provide you with analytics and progress tracking features.

3. Third-Party Services

The Application does not currently use third-party analytics or advertising services that collect data from your device.

4. Permissions

The Application may request certain permissions to function correctly (such as Haptic Feedback/Vibration). These permissions are used solely for the intended gameplay features and do not involve data collection.

5. Children's Privacy

The Application does not address anyone under the age of 13. Since we do not collect any personal data, we do not knowingly collect information from children.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Any changes are effective immediately upon being posted.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer through the contact information provided in the Play Store listing or via the main ARISE SOLO website.
