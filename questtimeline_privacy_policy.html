Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 28, 2026

1. Our Privacy Commitment

Quest Timeline: Daily Planner is built with a privacy-first philosophy. We believe your daily schedule and personal objectives are yours alone. Our policy is simple and transparent: We do not collect, store, or share your personal data.

Key takeaway: Your quests, timeline, and profile data live exclusively on your device. We have no "backend" or "cloud" where your information is stored.

2. No Data Collection

By design, Quest Timeline does not collect any personally identifiable information (PII). This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Your name, email address, or contact details.
  • Your location data.
  • Your quest titles, descriptions, or timing data.
  • Any usage statistics or analytics.

3. Local-First Storage

All data you input into Quest Timeline is stored locally on your device using encrypted, persistent storage (Hive). This data never leaves your device unless you manually perform a "Data Export" to back up your own information.

4. No Authentication Required

Quest Timeline does not require any account creation, login, or third-party authentication. You can use all features of the app immediately after installation without providing any personal credentials.

5. Third-Party Services

Quest Timeline does not utilize any third-party tracking, advertising SDKs (like AdMob), or analytics platforms (like Firebase Analytics). We do not share any data with third-party providers because we do not have any data to share.

6. Permissions

Quest Timeline may request basic system permissions (such as storage access) solely to facilitate the Import/Export functionality for your data. These permissions are used exclusively for locally-triggered actions and do not involve data transmission over the internet.

7. Contact

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or the security of your local data, you may contact us through the official support channel listed on our Play Store listing.
